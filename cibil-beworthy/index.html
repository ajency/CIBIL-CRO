How many digits are in your CIBIL score?

a One

Question 1/5

True or False: A few late payments won’t affect your CIBIL score.

a True
b False
Payment history is the most important factor in determining your credit score. Making on-time payments and keeping your level of debt at a reasonable level will help you improve your score. Late payments, on the other hand, will negatively affect your score.

Question 1/5

Myth or Fact: Checking your credit report will hurt your score.

a Myth
b Fact
Hard inquiries, such as applying for a credit card or a loan, could affect your score. Soft inquiries, like when you check your own score or order your free credit report, will not affect your score.
