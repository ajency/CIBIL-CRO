How to apply for an In-Principle loan offer?

Login to Partner's Website

  • Upon applying for loans tagged as In-Principle, you will be redirected to our partner’s website.
  • For security reasons, please re-enter your username and password.

Documentation and Eligibility

  • You will need 3 years of income tax proofs and 3 months latest salary slips, for verification.
  • Your personalized offers will be revised once your eligibility has been verified.

Approval Process

  • A letter of In-Principle approval will be generated once you select your preferred loan offer.
  • You can then approach the bank to initiate the KYC process and attach the In-Principle letter.

This digital approval will be subject to satisfactory due diligence and other reports as per requirements of the bank.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is CIBIL RANK and Company Credit Report (CCR)?

Company Credit Report (CCR) is a record of your company’s credit history. This is created from data submitted to CIBIL by lending institutions across India. CIBIL RANK, summarizes your CCR in the form of one number on a scale of 1 to 10, where 1 is the best rank. CIBIL Rank & Company Credit Report (CCR) is an indication of your loan eligibility.

How can I view my GST Report as a part of my Company Credit Report?

Your GST Report is now available along with your Company Credit Report (CCR) and CIBIL Rank to provide you with a 360° view of your business's financial wellness and credit health in one place. To access your GST Report, please use GSTIN authentication during registration.

How can the GST Report be useful for my business?

Your GST Report readily provides most of the information that banks seek to evaluate a loan application thereby accelerating the calculation of your business’ loan eligibility. Since the GST Report is based on data from the GST Portal, it acts as a trust-worthy data source and banks do not need to verify the authenticity of these reports.

How can I refresh the Company Credit Report, CIBIL Rank and GST Report?

Your CIBIL Rank and Company Credit Report can be refreshed every 7 days during the validity of the subscription. You can refresh your GST Report once every month for the duration of the subscription plan. Please note that you will need to complete the GSTIN-based authentication every time you refresh your GST Report.
