How to apply for an In principle loan offer

  • You will be redirected to our partner’s website when you apply for loans tagged as In-principle.
  • Please re-enter your username and password for security purposes.
  • The following documents are required for verification: 3 years of income tax proofs & 3 months latest salary slips.
  • Upon verification of your eligibility, you will receive a set of personalized offers and a letter of in principle approval based on your selection.
  • You can now approach the bank for the KYC process and attach the in principal letter.
