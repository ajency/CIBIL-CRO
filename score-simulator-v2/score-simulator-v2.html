How does the Score Simulator work?

Ever wondered how your credit behavior may affect your CIBIL Score and eventually impact your access to credit? The Score Simulator is tool that allows you to choose from various credit-related scenarios.

While your current CIBIL Score remains untouched, Score Simulator can help you take informed decisions and exercise discretion when making credit choices in the future.
